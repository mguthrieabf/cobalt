Security

When you login we know who you are and can provide a customised experience for you.

  • We can show menu options that you want and hide those that you don't
  • We can customise your dashboard to show information that is relevant to you
  • We can show your results without you having to search for them
  • Entering an event is a lot simpler because there is no need for you to input any identifying detail – the system already knows it is you

In addition, security means that only members can view information which is important for privacy.

Dashboard

On a large screen, the main menu is down the left and appears on every screen when you are logged in. On a smaller screen it appears on the top right of the screen as three horizonal lines.

At the top right is the search window, as well as indications that you have notifications or unpaid items in your shopping cart. The people icon to the far right provides access to your Profile, Settings or to logout.

At the top are three shortcuts – to your bridge credits account, your entries and your masterpoints.

The central part of the page will contain your personal results in future (not yet active) and a summary of your upcoming event bookings.

The remainder of the page contains Announcements and Discussions forums.

On a large screen, the main menu is down the left and appears on every screen when you are logged in. On a smaller screen it appears on the top right of the screen as three horizonal lines.

The main menu contains links to different parts of the application and is the primary means of navigating around the system.

The items in the menu are not the same for everyone – some players who have administrative access to certain areas will see additional items.

There are 3 shortcuts. These show a quick headline view of your bridge credit balance, your booked events and your masterpoints. Clicking on Bridge Credits takes you to the Payments page. Clicking on Masterpoints takes you to the Masterpoints page. Clicking on Booked events opens a separate list of all events you are currently entered in.

At the top right of the screen, key in your search term and click the search icon. search This brings up a list of results which can be filtered by areas of the system.

If you have any notifications there will be a bell icon notifications visible at the top right of the screen. The number in the red box is the number of notifications you have. Click to bring up a listing of the most recent notifications. At the bottom you can click “Show all notifications” to bring up full list – from which you can view them or delete them.

Notifications are temporary and will may be deleted after a few weeks.

If you have any unpaid entries, there will be a shopping cart icon shopping_cart visible at the top right of the screen. The number in the red box is the number of items you have in the cart. Click to bring up the Congress checkout page from where you can complete payment or delete the entry.
