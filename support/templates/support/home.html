Security

You can logout by clicking on the person icon at the top right hand corner of every screen. person.

Then choose Logout.

Always logout after using a shared computer.

You can change your password through your Profile which you can get to by clicking on the person icon at the top right hand corner of every screen. person.

You can also go to the Password Change Screen here.

Yes.

Here are some of the security features:

  • Data is encrypted at rest.
  • Data is encypted in transit using TLS1.2.
  • Production data is not used in any other environment e.g. Test.
  • The system is built in Django, one of the most secure web frameworks available.

There is a password reset option on the login screen. You will be sent a link in email to reset your password.
